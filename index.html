Welcome to my personal webpage!

My name is Lu Shaofeng. I am currently an Associate Professor with the Shien-Ming Wu School of Intelligent Engineering, South China University of Technology (SCUT). Before joining SCUT, I was a faculty member with Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University (XJTLU) from Sep. 2013 to Aug. 2019.

My research interests include railway traction systems, renewable power systems, meta-heuristic and numerical optimisation techniques, electric and hybrid driving systems, electric networks, power management strategies, energy storage devices and complex network.
